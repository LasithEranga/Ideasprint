What is Ideasprint ?

Ideasprint is an intra department ideathon for the undergraduates of Department of Industrial Management, University of Kelaniya. It was successfully held in the department premises last year and the top three teams were qualified to compete in the HackX-2019 finals. Every team was consisted with at least one mentor from MIT level 4 or past MITian who are highly experienced in Hackathons. They gave their fullest support to each and every team to triumph their ideas in the competition. Every team had unique ideas which inspired the honorable panel of judges. Ideasprint 2020 is under the thematic challenge with the innovative & sustainable solutions for COVID-19 outbreaks in current time.
