What is Ideasprint?

Ideasprint is an intra department ideathon for the undergraduates of Department of Industrial Management, University of Kelaniya. It was successfully held in the department premises last year and the top three teams were qualified to compete in the HackX-2019

Think


Think about a way to help society in this pandemic situation

Create a Team


Discuss your idea with team mates. Get help from lecturers mentors, and friends.

Join For Sessions


Sessions will be conducted by MITians who have gathered experiance in hackathons

Make Idea Happen


Teamwork makes ideas happen. Grab the trophy

TIMELINE

Come to The Introductory Sessions


Anyone who is interested can join the sessions. These sessions will be conducted by past MITians who are highly experienced in hackathons and similar competitions. Also, who are now in the industry.

Training with The Mentors


Teams will get the opportunity to discuss their ideas and improve it further with the assigned mentor.

Final Pitch


After 3 weeks of training with mentors and improving your idea, the Final pitch of IdeaSprint will be held. You will have to pitch your idea in-front of a panel of judges, which includes honorable lectures and Industry experts.

Path to hackX 2021


Top three winning teams of IDEASPRINT will get the opportunity to compete in the Hackx finals.
